PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION:

Use Google Maps for directions from your location. It includes the trolley and bus routes and timetables. If you’re at the Marriott, it’s two stops away on the trolley.

Buy a PRONTO fare card (for trolley & bus) at the airport or at the trolley station, or install their mobile app. Tap/scan it on a validator machine at each trolley platform before boarding, and on each bus upon boarding. Buses also accept cash (exact change).

  • Take the trolley (UC San Diego Blue Line) to UC San Diego Central Campus Station.
  • Or take bus routes 30 or 41 to Gilman Dr. & Myers Dr. (also called the Gilman Transit Center).

RIDE SHARE:
Set the location to “Ucsd Canyon View Pool, La Jolla, CA“. This is a passenger loading zone near the conference. Do not drop off or pick up on the road adjacent to the conference buildings.

Don’t use “UCSD” or “9500 Gilman Dr”; the whole campus uses that address for mailing purposes, but it won’t get you to the right place.

Alternate ride share locations: “Equality Ln, La Jolla” and “Justice Ln, La Jolla“.

The Lyft app may show a map/menu of designated UCSD drop-off locations; these are in Warren and are called Aquatic Center, Equality Ln, and Justice Ln.

VISITOR PARKING FOR FRIDAY MAY 20:

All UCSD parking lots require a UCSD parking permit, or visitor pay parking in selected lots. You may pay at a kiosk in each parking lot (cash or credit card) or install the ParkMobile app on your phone to pay online by credit card.

Enter UCSD at one of these locations:

  • On Regents Rd, enter at Health Sciences Dr., turn right at Voigt Dr., and right at Lot P704.
  • Or on Genesee Ave, enter at Campus Point Dr. and turn left on Voigt Dr.

Regents Road West Lot (surface parking lots):

  • Google Maps: “Regents Road West Lot, La Jolla, CA” 
  • Try Parking Lot P704 first. If it’s full, try adjacent lots P703 and P705.
  • Discount parking (“D” spots), flat rate $4/day, including UCSD personnel and visitors.
  • Pay at the pay station next to the bus stop, or by using the ParkMobile app on your phone. For ParkMobile, check signs for the Parking Zone code (4762).
  • Take the Regents Express bus to the Central Campus Station stop and walk the rest of the way. Face masks are required on the bus.
  • On return, you’ll take the Regents Express bus to the P703/P704 stop.

Athena Parking Structure:

  • Google Maps: “Athena Parking Structure”
  • This is a little closer but costs more.
  • Capped at $10/day in “Patient/Visitor” parking spots on the lower levels. Do not park in “A” or “B” permit spots.
  • Pay at a pay station in the parking structure, or by using the ParkMobile app on your phone. For ParkMobile, check signs for the Parking Zone code (4768).
  • Take the Regents Express bus to the Central Campus Station stop and walk the rest of the way. Face masks are required on the bus.
  • On return, you’ll take the Regents Express bus to the Health Sciences Walk (east) stop.

Gilman and Hopkins Parking Structures:

  • See info for Saturday May 21. These are closer to the conference, but on Friday, they cost up to $30 for the day.
VISITOR PARKING FOR SATURDAY MAY 21:

Gilman Parking Structure:

  • Google Maps: “Gilman Parking Structure”
  • Visitor parking (“V” spots) on the lower levels, $6 all day on weekends (or hourly rates on weekdays capped at $30/day).
  • Pay at a pay station in the parking structure, or by using the ParkMobile app on your phone. For ParkMobile, check signs for the Parking Zone code (4752).
  • See the walking directions above.

Hopkins Parking Structure:

  • Google Maps: “Hopkins Parking Structure”
  • Use the TOP entrance (on Voigt Dr.) instead of the GROUND FLOOR entrance (on Hopkins Dr.).
  • Visitor parking (“V” spots) on the roof, $6 all day on weekends (or hourly rates on weekdays capped at $30/day).
  • Pay at a pay station in the parking structure, or by using the ParkMobile app on your phone. For ParkMobile, check signs for the Parking Zone code (4752).
  • See the walking directions above.
